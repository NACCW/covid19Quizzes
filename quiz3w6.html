Week 7 : Quiz 3

All of the questions will be based on the message announced by the President on the 09th of April

       



1. How many weeks of lockdown has it been on the day (09 April 2020) when the president delivered the message?

Answer: 2 Weeks

2. What 3 main national Covid-19 interventions have caused our economy to ground to a halt?

Answer: Closing of borders to the world, children not being in schools and businesses closed their operations

3. According to the president,have South Africans respected the lockdown and largely observed the regulations?

Answer: Yes

4. What is the president’s wish to South Africans for our cooperation, for our commitment and above all for our patience?

Answer:A wish to thank us all, personally

5. The president’s wish is to thank us (South African) for what exactly ?

Answer: For reaffirming to each other and to the world that we South Africans are a people who come together and unite at moments of great crisis

6. Earlier today (09 April 2020), who did he have a productive meeting about the work they are doing in provinces and districts to stop the spread of the virus?

Answer: With our Premiers

7. Who else did he have a discussion with ,who collectively pledged their support for the efforts that are being made to combat the pandemic?

Answer: The leaders of all our political parties represented in Parliament

8. What evidence is sufficient to show that the lockdown is working in South Africa?

Answer All mentioned

9. What is the president’s ask to all of us (South African) that has to do with making even greater sacrifices so that our country may survive this crisis and so that tens of thousands of lives may be saved?

Answer To endure the extension of nation-wide lockdown by a further two weeks beyond the initial 21 days

10. Which Council has decided to extend the nation-wide lockdown by a further two weeks beyond the initial 21 days?

Answer:National Coronavirus Command Council
