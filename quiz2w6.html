Week 6 : Quiz 2

All of the questions will be based on Lockdown fitness tutorials (from the 02nd of April until 09th of April ) shown in the SA Corona Virus Online Portal

       



1. How many tutorial techniques are shown by the 9th of April?

Answer:8

2. Are these exercises only for females?

Answer: No

3. What is the aim of these exercise tutorials?

Answer: To teach us how to stay active at home during the lockdown

4. Who is giving the instructions to these exercises and from which fitness company does he come from?

Answer: Coach Fu from FT fitness

5. According to Coach Fu, do you have to squeeze your legs together when you do push ups and why?

Answer: Yes, so you can keep your entire body tight

6. Which exercise starts off by keeping feet and shoulders off the floor?

Answer: V-ups

7. Which 2 exercises have almost a similar starting position?

Answer: Push ups and mountain climber

8. Which exercise needs you to have a chair or something to balance on?

AnswerDip

9. Which exercise will work the entire body and the lungs and it starts by lying down flat?

AnswerBurpee

10. Are all of these exercises only for those who have tested positive to the coronavirus?

Answer:No
