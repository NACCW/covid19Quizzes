Week 4 : Quiz

Most of the questions will be based on the statements addressed by the President on the escalation of measures to combat COVID-19 epidemic.

       



1.Besides the group of healthcare workers and other essential public servants, which other group will be exempted from the lockdown ?

Answer: All mentioned

2. Under which 3 strictly controlled circumstances will individuals be allowed to leave their homes during the national lock-down?

Answer: When leaving to seek medical care, buy food, medicine and other supplies or collect a social grant

3. How will the government provide main support for homeless people during this lock-down?

Answer: Temporary shelters that meet the necessary hygiene standards will be identified for them to use

4. Besides the homeless people, who else can use the sites identified for quarantine and self-isolation?

Answer: Those who cannot self-isolate at home

5. Which type of shops and businesses will be NOT be closed during this national lock-down?

Answer: All mentioned

6. Which companies need to make arrangements for care and maintanance to avoid damage during this national lock-down ?

Answer:Companies whose operations require continuous processes such as furnaces and underground mine operations

7. Will provision be made for essential transport services to continue, including transport for essential staff and for patients who need to be managed elsewhere?

Answer:Yes

8. Why is the nation-wide lockdown in South Africa necessary ?

AnswerTo fundamentally disrupt the chain of transmission across society

9. Which team is deployed to support the South African Police Service in ensuring that the measures announced are implemented?

Answer South African National Defence Force

10. Which programme will accompy the nationwide lockdown and what will be it's main tasks?

Answer:The public health management programme which will significantly increase screening, testing, contact tracing and medical management
