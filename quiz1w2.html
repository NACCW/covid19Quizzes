Week 2 : Easy Quiz

Most of the questions will be based on the brief from the Alliance for Child Protection in Humanitarian Action and the Ministerial briefings on the impacts of Covid19 in Education

       



1. What type of work is done by Alliance for Child Protection in Humanitarian Action?

Answer: As a global interagency group focused on child protection in humanitarian settings. Their work includes setting standards and providing technical support to ensure theat efforts to protection children from violence and exploitation are effective and of high quality .

2. According to brief from the Alliance for Child Protection in Humanitarian actions, what 3 recommended covid19 measures can all negatively impact children and their families?

Answer: Home-based, facility-based and zonal-based quarantine and isolation

3. What is the aim of the Alliance for Child Protection in Humanitarian actions brief?

Answer: To support child protection practitioners to better respond to the child protection risks during a COVID-19 pandemic.

4. According to the brief, what are the 2 main quarantine measures imposed on children globally?

Answer: School closures and restrictions on movements

5. On which date did the Basic Education department determined that schools should resume?

Answer:14 April 2020

6. Should there be changes to this date, who will the department communicate with?

Answer:Parents of the learners

7. Who is the minister of Basic Education?

Answer:Ms Angelina Angie Matsie Motshekga

8. The department of Basic Education's directive on Covid19 only affects public schools and not private schools?

AnswerNot True, it affects both public and private schools.
